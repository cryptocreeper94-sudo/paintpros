# PaintPros.io by Orbit

## Platform Overview

PaintPros.io is a white-label SaaS platform designed for painting contractors and home service businesses. It provides complete business management including interactive estimating tools, crew scheduling, customer portals, and automated marketing - all wrapped in a franchisable multi-tenant architecture.

## Tagline

*"The complete painting business platform that runs while you paint."*

## Short Description (50 words)

PaintPros.io automates painting contractor operations with instant estimation, crew management, customer booking, and AI-powered marketing. White-label ready for franchise deployment with role-based dashboards. Built for painting companies who want to scale from solo operator to multi-crew enterprise.

## Medium Description (150 words)

PaintPros.io is the comprehensive business platform that transforms how painting contractors operate. From the moment a lead comes in to the final invoice, PaintPros automates every step of the customer journey.

The platform features an interactive room estimator with real-time pricing, a 5-step booking wizard for customers, GPS-verified crew time tracking, and AI-powered proposal generation. The built-in weather system prevents scheduling conflicts, while the messaging hub keeps crews coordinated.

For business owners, PaintPros provides role-based dashboards (Owner, Admin, Crew Lead), automated marketing through TrustLayer Marketing integration, and a customer portal with project galleries. The franchise-ready architecture allows white-label deployment for multiple territories.

PaintPros connects to the Orbit Ecosystem for payroll sync, cross-product analytics, and unified customer data across all Trust Layer applications.
