VedaSolus is a production-ready, mobile-first holistic health platform that bridges Eastern healing traditions (Ayurveda, Traditional Chinese Medicine) with Western scientific wellness. We replace fragmented health tracking with integrated mind-body insights, AI-powered guidance, and a community of verified practitioners.

What We Offer:

For Seekers (Users):
- Personal Health Tracking - Sleep, nutrition, exercise, and heart rate logging with visual analytics
- Dosha Analysis - Ayurvedic constitution assessment with personalized recommendations
- AI Wellness Coach - GPT-4 powered guidance with ElevenLabs voice synthesis
- Health Passport - Digital wellness identity with QR verification
- Daily Rituals - Time-based Ayurvedic routines (Brahma Muhurta, Kapha/Pitta/Vata periods)
- Educational Library - Bridge Eastern philosophy with Western science
- Community Tribes - Connect with like-minded wellness seekers

For Practitioners:
- Practice Hub Dashboard - Client management, appointments, and business analytics
- Orbit Payroll Integration - Automated timesheet sync and payment processing
- Client Scheduling - Appointment booking with status tracking
- Performance Metrics - Revenue, consultations, and client retention analytics
- Staff Management - 1099/W-2 contractor and employee tracking
- Certification Tracking - License and credential management

For Administrators:
- Full System Control - User management, content moderation, system health
- Analytics Dashboard - Platform-wide metrics and engagement tracking
- Developer Tools - API access, database management, system diagnostics
- Partner Portal - PIN-secured access for stakeholders

Enterprise Features:
- Firebase Authentication - Google Sign-In with branded experience
- Multi-Role Architecture - Seeker, Practitioner, Admin, Developer dashboards
- Orbit Staffing Integration - Full ecosystem sync for payroll and revenue
- Stripe Payments - Subscription management and practitioner payments
- Dark Wave Smart Chain - Blockchain-verified health credentials (roadmap)

Premium Features:
- Voice-Enabled AI Coach - Natural conversation with ElevenLabs synthesis
- Video Consultations - ZEGOCLOUD/Daily.co telehealth integration (roadmap)
- FHIR/HealthKit Sync - Medical record and device integration (roadmap)
- NFT Health Badges - Blockchain achievement verification (roadmap)
