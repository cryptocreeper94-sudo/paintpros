## **Orby Commander - Venue Operations Platform**









Tagline: Unify your venue operations with one powerful command center.



Description:

Orby Commander is a comprehensive Progressive Web App (PWA) designed to transform how venues manage their operations. Built for stadiums, arenas, and large event spaces, Orby replaces fragmented communication systems with a single, unified digital platform that puts real-time control at your fingertips.



Key Features:



Emergency Command Center - All-seeing operations dashboard for instant incident response and priority escalation

Delivery Lifecycle Tracking - Full visibility from request to delivery (Requested → Approved → Picking → On the Way → Delivered)

Three-Phase Inventory Counting - Digital templates, AI-powered scanning with OCR, and Manager Document Hub

Alcohol Compliance Monitoring - Real-time alerts for regulatory inspections and violation tracking

GPS-Guided Navigation - Interactive stadium maps with walking directions

Real-Time Team Communications - Unified messaging across all departments with smart routing

Multi-Role Access System - 12+ specialized dashboards for every role from Stand Lead to Executive

Sandbox Training Mode - Safe environment for staff onboarding without affecting live operations

Offline-Ready PWA - Works even when connectivity drops

Target Users:

Operations Managers, Supervisors, Stand Leads, Warehouse Teams, Kitchen Staff, IT Support, Compliance Officers, and Executive Leadership
