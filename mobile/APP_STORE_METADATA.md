### Description
PaintPros is the ultimate mobile companion for professional painting services. Get instant estimates for your painting projects, connect with trusted local painters, and manage your home improvement needs with blockchain-verified transparency.

• Instant Estimates - Get accurate pricing for interior and exterior painting projects in seconds
• Room Calculator - Calculate costs for walls, trim, ceilings, and doors

• Role-Based Dashboards - Access for admins, owners, managers, and teams
• Professional Network - Connect with licensed, insured painting professionals

Whether you're a homeowner planning a refresh or a painting company managing quotes, PaintPros streamlines the entire estimation process with modern technology and trusted security.
