# Privacy Policy for PaintPros Mobile App

**Last Updated: December 13, 2025**

## Introduction

PaintPros ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Contact Information**: Email address when requesting estimates
- **Project Details**: Square footage, room details, and service selections for estimates
- **Account Credentials**: PIN codes for dashboard access (stored locally on device)

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data**: App feature usage, pages visited
- **Analytics**: Anonymous usage statistics to improve the app

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide painting estimates and quotes
- Process service requests
- Communicate about your projects
- Improve our app and services
- Send promotional communications (with your consent)

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely on our servers
- We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission
- PIN codes are stored locally on your device only
- We retain your data only as long as necessary

## Blockchain Verification

Our app uses Solana blockchain technology to:
- Create tamper-proof records of estimates and documents
- Provide verifiable proof of document authenticity
- All blockchain transactions are public and permanent

## Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services including:
- Payment processors (Stripe, Coinbase)
- Analytics providers
- Cloud hosting services

These services have their own privacy policies.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt-out of marketing communications
- Withdraw consent at any time

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email.

## Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:
- **Email**: privacy@paintpros.io
- **Website**: https://paintpros.io/privacy

## California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA:
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of sale of personal information
- Right to non-discrimination

We do not sell your personal information.
