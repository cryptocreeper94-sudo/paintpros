## Executive Summary

**PaintPros.io** is a multi-tenant SaaS platform designed specifically for the painting and home services industry. Built by Orbit, it provides franchise-ready, white-label websites for painting companies with modern design, intelligent estimating tools, comprehensive CRM capabilities, and blockchain-verified documentation.

### Vision
To become the premier digital platform powering painting businesses across North America, enabling small-to-medium painting contractors to operate with enterprise-level efficiency and customer experience.

### Mission
Empower painting professionals with cutting-edge technology that streamlines operations, generates qualified leads, and builds trust through transparency and innovation.

### Target Market
1. **Primary:** Independent painting contractors ($500K-$5M annual revenue)

3. **Tertiary:** Home services companies expanding into painting

- Outdated websites that don't convert visitors

## Go-To-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Beta (Current)
- **Target:** 10 beta clients in Tennessee
- **Goal:** Validate product-market fit
- **Pricing:** Free during beta, commitment to paid tier
- **Current Client:** Nashville Painting Professionals (NPP)

### Phase 2: Regional Launch (Q1 2025)
- **Target:** Southeast U.S. (TN, GA, AL, KY)
- **Goal:** 50 paying customers
- **Strategy:** 
  - Partner with paint suppliers (Sherwin-Williams)
  - Attend industry trade shows
  - Content marketing (painting business tips)

### Phase 3: National Expansion (Q3 2025)
- **Target:** National presence
- **Goal:** 500 customers
- **Strategy:**
  - Franchise partnerships
  - Referral program launch
  - Paid advertising (Google, Facebook)

### Phase 4: Franchise System (Q1 2026)
- **Target:** Painting franchise systems
- **Goal:** 5 franchise partnerships (50+ locations each)
- **Strategy:**
  - Enterprise sales team
  - Custom integration packages
  - White-label reseller program
