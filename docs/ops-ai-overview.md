# Ops AI - Your Business In Your Pocket

## What Is It?

**Ops AI** is a voice-controlled business assistant app that lets you manage Nashville Painting Professionals operations from your phone. Just speak to it and it takes action.

---

## How To Access It

1. Go to: **[your-domain]/ops** on your phone
2. Add it to your home screen (it works like a native app)
3. Enter your PIN
4. Start talking

---

## What Can It Do?

### Scheduling & Calendar
- "What's on the schedule for today?"
- "Move the Johnson appointment to Thursday at 2pm"
- "Show me tomorrow's jobs"
- "Is there availability next Tuesday afternoon?"

### Customer & Leads
- "Show me all pending leads"
- "Look up the Smith estimate"
- "Who contacted us this week?"
- "What's the status on the Riverside project?"

### Crew Management  
- "Where are all the crews right now?"
- "Assign Mike's crew to the downtown job"
- "Who's working tomorrow?"
- "Check on the Franklin job progress"

### Business Reports
- "What's our revenue this week?"
- "How many estimates are pending?"
- "Show me the monthly summary"
